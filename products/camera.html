    The CAMERA was our answer to the search for the absolute perfect camera bag. The features of this bag were inspired by our in-house photographer Zoe to be perfect no matter the trip. Extra thick padding in a semi-rigid body provides a safe environment for your most treasured gear.


    The CAMERA can be stored inside our Imperial Backpacks or used on it's own - carried by handle or over the shoulder. It can even be used as a backpack with our TRANSFORMER KIT. This bag can also carry 3 Exterior Capsules of it's own like the DAILY, WATER, ROLL and LENS.


    Features:


    Coated 900d Cordura® exterior


    Waterproof YKK® Zippers with Blackout ZOX Pulls


    Semi-rigid construction with .5" padding throughout


    Easy - clean crushed velvet lining with padded movable dividers


    Zipper - separated gear compartment with 3 mesh zipper pockets


    Built - in filter sleeves and memory card compartments


    Hidden 10" x 8" tablet compartment


    Interchangeable Tensions Straps with included EXTENDER Shoulder pad


    3 - sided expansion brackets to attach 3 additional exterior Capsules


    Velcro backed for easy mounting in both V1 and V2 Imperial Backpacks


    Reinforced EXPERIENCE EVERYTHING carry handle


    TRANSFORMER KIT enabled


    ZOXLOCK compatible for extra security



    This is a Certified ZOX Capsule with a serial number laser cut into the tag.
