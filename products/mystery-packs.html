  • Story

    Our most popular pack of all time, with good reason! Mystery Packs are our way of incorporating a game of chance into ZOX. Each pack contains 3 completely random Straps that range from in stock to sold-out.

    All Mystery Packs include the chance to find the rarest designs we make: Black Stars and Galactics. These beauties have a rarity level of Grail and only have 100 pieces made. They're easily identifiable by the foil cards that come included inside their packaging. You can collect 5 of those cards and redeem them for something extra special by following the instructions here.

    Mystery Packs also include a lot of other exclusive Straps not available anywhere else on the site.

    1. White Stars - Limited to just 1,000 pieces made, White Stars are identifiable by the small white star on the inside of the band and in the top right corner of the collector's card.

    2. Mini-Collections - Limited to just 2,000 pieces made, Mini-Collections changed up every month. They're most easily identified by the symbol at the top right corner, as well as inside the Strap. Mini-Collections are designed in sets of 7. If you're one of the first 200 people to collect all 6, you can send in your collector cards here for that set and we'll send the 7th one back to you, along with your cards. This 7th design is 1 of 200 pieces and is referred to as the Moonstone. It comes with multi-color stitching and a matching black and gold pin.

    At any given point there are around 250 different designs being mixed into Mystery Packs, with what's being mixed in constantly changing.

    Please note: Due to the ultra-collectible nature of Mystery Packs, once the packages are opened, they are non-refundable.
