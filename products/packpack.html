    This is special backpack I designed for my son Sven. When traveling, I noticed the cheap backpacks we would buy for him consistently broke - one we had just bought for travel actually broke before we even got off the plane, meaning I had to carry it by hand for the duration of the trip. That moment inspired me to create something better - we call it the PackPack. This bag is durable enough to handle anything kids put it through while also easily attaching to our V2 Imperial bag when they're too tired to carry it themselves.
